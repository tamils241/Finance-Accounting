Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new client?
Navigate to the Clients page from the sidebar. Click the "Add Client" button in the top-right corner. Fill in the client details including name, email, company, and contact information. Click "Save" to create the new client profile. The client will appear in your client list immediately.
How to generate reports?
Go to the Reports page from the sidebar. You will see available report types including Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and more. Select the report type you need, choose the date range, and click "Generate". The report will be generated and can be downloaded as PDF or exported to Excel.
How to process payroll?
Access the Payroll section from the sidebar navigation. Review the pending employees list and verify salary details. Click "Process Payroll" to initiate the batch processing. The system will calculate deductions, taxes, and net pay automatically. Confirm the payroll run to distribute payments to employees.
How to file taxes?
Navigate to the Tax Planning page from the sidebar. Select the appropriate tax service (Federal, State, or GST/VAT). Review the tax summary and ensure all financial data is up to date. Follow the guided filing workflow to complete and submit your tax return. You will receive a confirmation once the filing is submitted successfully.
How to invite team members?
Go to Settings and find the "Team Management" section. Click the "Invite" button and enter the email addresses of the team members you want to add. Assign appropriate roles (Admin, Manager, Staff) and set permissions. Click "Send Invitation" — they will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.
