Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the "My Invoices" page from the sidebar. Click on any invoice row and select the "Download" option. You can download invoices in PDF format for your records.
Go to Settings > Billing section. Click "Update Card" to modify your payment information. All changes are securely saved and your next billing cycle will use the updated method.
Tax documents are typically available 30 days before each filing deadline. You can check the "Tax Documents" page for the latest status and due dates for all your filings.
Visit the "Financial Reports" page and click the "Generate Report" button on any report card. Reports are generated in real-time and can be downloaded as PDF or exported to CSV format.
